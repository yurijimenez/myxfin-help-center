# Customer Groupings

Customer Groupings in MyxFin allows users to organize customers into logical groups for easier management and reporting. This module helps categorize customers based on business needs, such as by type, region, or other custom criteria.

---

### How to Create a New Groupings:

**A. Rename the Group Label**

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **System Settings**
3. Go to **Parameters Tab**
4. Under Customers Groupings Enter the label for each group you needed. 
5. Press Enter after you type the label.
6. Check mark will appear beside the Group to indicate that the change is saved.  

![Navigating to CustomerGroup](/assets/img/custgroup.png)

---

**B. Add Group Options** 

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Customers**
3. Click **Groupings** 
4. Click **Create New (F1)** 
5. Enter the details of the new option. 
6. Click **Add Details**
