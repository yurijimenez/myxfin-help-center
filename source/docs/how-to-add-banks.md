# Bank Masterlist

A "bank" typically refers to a financial institution where a business holds its financial accounts, such as 
checking accounts, savings accounts, and other banking-related activities. Banks play a crucial role in 
financial transactions, payment processing, and managing the financial aspects of sales and inventory 
operations. Cash Flow Management: By tracking incoming and outgoing payments, the system 
contributes to managing the business's cash flow, helping ensure sufficient funds are available to cover 
expenses. 

### How to Create a New Banks:
1. Go to **Master Data Files**
2. Click **Accounting Parameters**
3. Click **Banks**
4. Click **Create New (F1)** 
5. Enter the details of the new bank. 
6. Click **Save (F2)**

### How to Update the banks:

1. Go to **Master Data Files**
2. Click **Accounting Parameters** 
3. Click **Banks**
4. Select a bank and click the blue font text or Bank Code. 
5. When the bank details appear, Click Edit (CTRL+E). 
6. Enter the new details of the bank. 
7. Click **Save (CTRL+S)**

### How to inactive the banks:

1. Go to **Master Data Files**
2. Click **Accounting Parameters**
3. Click **Banks**
4. Click the **yellow cycle icon** or **Inactive**

o Click the **green cycle icon** or **Active** to activate the item.
