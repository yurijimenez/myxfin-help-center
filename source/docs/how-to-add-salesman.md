# Salesman

A **"salesman"** typically refers to an individual or entity responsible for selling products or services to 
customers. The role of a salesman can vary depending on the organization. 

--- 

### How to Create New Salesman :

1. Go to **Master Data** Files 
2. Click **Salesman**
3. Click **Create New**
4. Input details on all necessary fields. 
5. Click **Save**
