# Units Of Measure

The Units of Measure (UOM) module in MyxFin allows users to define and manage measurement units for inventory items. This ensures consistency when recording quantities for purchases, sales, and stock management.

---

![Navigating to Uom](/assets/img/uom.png)

### How to Create a New Unit of Measure:
1. Go to**Master Data Files** 
2. Click**Items** 
3. Click**Unit of Measure**
4. Click**Create New (F1)**
5. Enter the details of the new UOM. 
6. Click**Add Details**

---

### How to Update the Unit of Measure :

1. Go to**Master Data Files**
2. Click**Items**
3. Click**Unit of Measure**
4. Select**uom**and click the**blue font text**or the**UOM Code**. 
5. Enter the updated details for the UOM 
6. Click **Update**Detail.

![Navigating to Uom](/assets/img/uom1.png)

---

### How to Active/Inactive Unit of Measure:

1. Go to **Master Data Files**
2. Click **Items**
3. Click **Unit of Measure** 
4. Click **Active**(Green)/**Inactive**(Yellow) icon.  

![Navigating to Uom](/assets/img/uom2.png)
