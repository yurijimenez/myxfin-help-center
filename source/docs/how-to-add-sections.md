# Sections

Sections define the inventory locations or categories referenced in inventory transactions such as 
transfers, physical counts, and inventory adjustments. They help ensure accurate tracking and control of 
stock movements within the system.  

---

### How to Create a New Section:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Sections**
3. Click **Create New (F1)**
4. Enter the details of the new section. 
5. Click **Save (F2)**

![Navigating to Section](/assets/img/section.png)

### How to Update the Section:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files
2. Click **Sections** 
3. Select a section and click the blue font text or Section Name. 
4. Enter the new details of the section. 
5. Click **Save (CTRL+S)**

![Navigating to Section](/assets/img/section1.png)

### How to Inactive the Section:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Sections**
3. Click the **yellow cycle** icon or **Inactive**

![Navigating to Section](/assets/img/section2.png)
