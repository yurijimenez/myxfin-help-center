# Bank Deposit

Bank Deposit is used to record and manage deposits from cash or customer payments into your bank accounts in MyxFin. This feature allows users to create new deposit transactions, edit existing ones, or void incorrect entries. Bank Deposit ensures that your cash inflows are properly documented and reconciled with your bank accounts.

---

![Navigating to Bank Deposit](/assets/img/bankdepo.png)

1. **Create New F1**Click to create new transaction. 
2. **Void Transaction**Click to void transaction. 
3. Filtering parameters are used to search or filter your data . 

![Navigating to Bank Deposit](/assets/img/bankdepo1.png)

> 💡 **Take note:**This module will on be used depends on the recording of the receive payments. 

> 💡 **Take note:** If the Receive Payment Deposit to Account value is your Cash on Hand Account. You can use this module record your bank deposits.
