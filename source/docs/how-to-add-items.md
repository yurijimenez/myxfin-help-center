# Item Masterlist

The "Items" refer to the individual products, goods, or items that a business sells or manages as part of 
its inventory. Each unique product that a company offers for sale is categorized and tracked as an "item" 
within the system. Items play a crucial role in managing inventory, processing sales orders, tracking 
stock levels, and providing accurate financial information.

### How to Create a New Item:
1. Go to **Master Data Files** 
2. Click **Items**
3. Click **Master list**
4. Click **Create New (F1)**
5. Enter the details of the new item. 
6. Click **Save (CTRL+S)**

### How to Update the Items:

1. Go to **Master Data Files**
2. Click **Items**
3. Click **Master List**
4. Select item and click the blue font text or the Part No/Item Code. 
5. Click **Edit (CTRL+E)**
6. Enter the updated details for the item.  
7. Click **Save (CTRL+S)**

### How to inactive the Items:

1. Go to **Master Data Files**
2. Click **Items**
3. Click **Master List**
4. Click the **yellow cycle icon** or **Inactive**
5. Click the **green cycle icon** or **Active** to activate the item.
