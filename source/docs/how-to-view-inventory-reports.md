# Inventory Reports

The Inventory module provides a centralized view of your stock levels and asset distribution across different departments and locations.

![Inventory Reports](/assets/img/inventreport.png)

### 1. Inventory Summary Report
This report provides a comprehensive overview of your current stock on hand, allowing you to monitor availability and warehouse movements.

| Report Name | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Inventory Summary** | Displays current stock levels, quantities, and values across various sections. |

---

### 2. Typical Parameters
To generate an accurate inventory view, configure the following filters:

* **Date Range:** Select the "From" and "To" dates to view stock status for a specific period. 

* **Section:** Choose the specific department or location you wish to audit.
 
**Examples:** `DEVELOPERS`, `TECH SUPPORT`, `TECHNICAL DEPARTMENT`, `STORE 1`, `CAFE`, etc.

---

### 3. Action Buttons
* **View Report:** Click this button to render the inventory data directly on your screen.

![Inventory Reports](/assets/img/inventreport1.png)

---

> 💡 **Usage Tip:** Selecting a specific section like **"STORE 1"** or **"TECH SUPPORT"** helps narrow down the list, making it easier to conduct physical spot checks for specific teams or branches.
