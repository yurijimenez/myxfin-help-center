# Sales Pricelist

The Sales Price List, also known as the Price Matrix, stores structured pricing rules for items. It ensures consistent and accurate pricing across all sales transactions in the ERP system. 

---

### How to View and Add New PM Versions:

1. Go to**Master Data**Files. 
2. Click**Price List**
3. Click**Sale Pricelist**
4. Click**PM Version**
5. Click**Add New**
6. Enter the**new Version Code**and**Version Description**. 

o **To delete** specific PM Version, just click delete.

o **To update**, just enter the updated details of Version Description
.Only the Version Description can be update.

7. Click Save Details. 

![Navigating to SP](/assets/img/sp.png)

---

### How to Add New Price Matrix :

1. Go to**Master Data**Files. 
2. Click**Price List**
3. Click**Sale Pricelist**
4. Click**Create New (F1)**
5. Select**PM Version**
6. Click**Proceed**
7. Enter the Description and Effect Date. 
8. Enter the product code or description to select the item. 
9. Enter the price matrix of the item. 
10. Click**Save**
11. Click**Post**

### How to Update the Price Matrix :

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Price List**
3. Click **Sale Pricelist**
4. Select PM Batch No. and click the blue text icon. 
5. Click **Edit**
6. Enter the new details of the price matrix. 
7. Click **Save (CTRL+S)**

o Once the status is Posted,**editing** is **not allowed**
