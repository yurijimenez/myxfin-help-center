# System Settings

A "system setup" refers to the process of configuring and customizing the various settings, parameters, and options within the software to align it with the specific needs and processes of the business. System setup involves defining how the system will operate, what data will be captured, and how various features and functionalities will be used. Proper system setup is crucial to ensure that the software functions accurately and efficiently according to the organization's requirements. 

---

### How to Setup or Update Company Information:

1. Go to **Master Data Files** 
2. Click **System Setup**
3. Click **Company Details**
4. Enter the company information. 
5. Click **Save** Company Details. 

![Navigating to System Settings](/assets/img/sysettings.png)

---

### How to Setup Document Serial Numbers:

This functionality ensures compliance with BIR CAS requirements on serial numbering of receipts and 
invoices, including the use of a minimum of six (6) running digits with leading zeroes and a clearly 
defined numbering convention. 

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Document Serial Numbers**
3. Click **Start New Year** 
4. Edit the serial numberings as needed 

![Navigating to System Settings](/assets/img/sysettings1.png)

> 💡 **Take note:**The Start New Year function initializes the document serial number series for all transactions. Editing is allowed only within the Document Serial Number module. Upon exit, the system automatically saves and locks the series to prevent further modification.  

---

### How to Setup the Base Currency:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files.  
2. Click **System Setup**
3. Click **Parameters** 
4. Select Automatic or Manual from the drop-down options.

![Navigating to System Settings](/assets/img/sysettings2.png)

--- 

### How to Add/Update Contacts Details Custom Field:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **System Setup** 
3. Click **Parameters**

4. Click **Add** to Add New Field or Edit the Description of an existing field. 

5. Click Save Contact Details to save the changes. 

> ✔️ You may also activate or deactivate a field by clicking the button on the right side.  

![Navigating to System Settings](/assets/img/sysettings3.png)
