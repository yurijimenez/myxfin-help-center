# Payment Terms

Payment Terms define the payment conditions applied to customer and supplier transactions. These terms control due date calculation, payment schedules, and aging in the ERP system. 

### How to Create a New Payment Terms:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Accounting Parameters** 
3. Click **Payment Terms** 
4. Click **Create New (F1)** 
5. Enter the details of the Payment Terms. 
6. Click **Save**

![Navigating to Payment Term](/assets/img/npayment.png) 

---

### How to Update a Payment Term:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Accounting Parameters** 
3. Click **Payment Terms** 
4. Click the **yellow button** 
5. Update the details of the Payment Terms. 
6. Click **Update** 

![Navigating to Payment Term](/assets/img/npayment1.png)
