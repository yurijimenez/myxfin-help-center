# Item Groupings

### Create New Transaction:

**A. Rename the Group Label**

1. Go to**Master Data**Files. 
2. Click **System Settings**
3. Go to **Parameters**Tab. 
4. Under Items Groupings Enter the label for each group you needed. 
5. Press**Enter**after you type the label.
6. A check mark will appear beside the Group to indicate that 
the change is saved. 

![Navigating to Itemgroup](/assets/img/gr1.png)

---

**B. Add Group Options**

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Items**
3. Click **Groupings**
4. Click **Create New (F1)**
5. Enter the details of the new option. 
6. Click **Add Details**

![Navigating to Itemgroup](/assets/img/gr2.png)
