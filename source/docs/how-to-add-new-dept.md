# New Department

Departments define organizational units used as cost centers in the ERP system. They are referenced in transactions to accurately allocate and monitor costs and expenses.

---

### How to Create a New Department :

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Department**
3. Click **Create New (F1)**
4. Enter the details of the new Department. 
5. Click **Add Detail** 

![Navigating to dept](/assets/img/dept.png)

---

### How to Update the Department:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Departments** 
3. Select a section and click the blue font text or Department Code. 
4. Enter the new details of the department. 
5. Click **Update Detail** 

![Navigating to dept](/assets/img/dept1.png)

---

### How to Inactive the Department:

1. Go to **Master Data** Files. 
2. Click **Departments**
3. Click the **yellow cycle** icon or **Inactive**

o Click the **green cycle** icon or **Active** to activate the item.
